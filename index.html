Ramen 101

What is Ramen?

Ramen is a type of Japanese noodle soup. Once a simple street food, it's now become a gourmet phenomenon around the world.

Every bowl of ramen has three main components:

  1. Broth:A good bowl of ramen begins with a hearty flavorful broth.
  2. Noodles:Ramen noodles contain wheat flour, salt, water and a special alkaline water called "Kansui".
  3. Toppings:Ramen chefs artfully arrange each bowl with toppings like fried veggies, sliced meat and sheets of seaweed before serving.
