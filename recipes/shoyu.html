Shoyu Ramen

Ingredients

  • Fresh Ramen Noodles
  • Sesame Oil
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Soy Sauce
  • Cooking Sake
  • Dashi
  • Mirin
  • Ramen Water
  • Ramen Egg (Optional)
  • Menma
  • Dry nori sheets
  • Chopped Scallion
  • Ground White Pepper

How to Make Shoyu Ramen

  1. Gather all kitchen tools and ingredients.
  2. Add broth flavoring elements: Soy sauce, sake, dash etc. Once it's boiling lower the heat, cover and simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. In a separate pot boil the noodles. Reserve a bit of the noodle water for soup base.
  4. Add noodles, soup and toppings together.
